Fish Taco Slaw

Ingredients

  • Cucumber
  • Cabbage
  • Carrot
  • Apple
  • Yogurt
  • Lemon
  • Honey
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Mix yogurt, lemon, olive oil, and honey.
  2. Chop cucumber, cabbage, carrot, and apple.
  3. Combine chopped fruits and veggies in a bowl and add yogurt sauce, salt, and pepper
